A/B Testing

A/B Testing has been an essential part of how I make informed, data-backed decisions in digital marketing. I’ve used it to test and refine various website and content elements — like CTAs, headlines, meta descriptions, and email subject lines.

While working on projects like CreatedByLonna and Performance Max, I ran A/B tests to optimize click-through rates and engagement using real performance data. Tools like Google Optimize, heatmaps, and CTR analysis helped me understand which versions resonated most with users.

A/B Testing taught me the importance of experimentation, iteration, and continuous improvement — especially in SEO, content marketing, and user experience strategy.

I look forward to using this approach even more in future campaigns to refine marketing efforts and drive better results.
