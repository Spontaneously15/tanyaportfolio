Canva

Canva is one of my favorite tools for visual content creation. I’ve used it to design everything from social media graphics and presentations to email banners and infographics — all while maintaining brand consistency and visual appeal.

I’ve created assets for internal newsletters, blog visuals, and promotional materials during my time at Montclair State University, Performance Max, and CreatedByLonna. I love how intuitive and efficient Canva is for turning ideas into professional-looking designs.

I’ve worked with both templates and custom layouts, and I enjoy experimenting with typography, color palettes, and design flow to create content that’s visually engaging and effective.

Whether it’s for social media posts, reports, or email campaigns — Canva always helps me deliver clean, consistent, and creative visuals.
